Sleepers

Summary

A research project at Social Body Lab. Sleepers are designed to keep the user from staying up too late. It will send messages of “It’s time to go to bed” continuously to the user after a preset time. Only when the user goes to the bed and not wearing Sleepers, the user’s phone will stop getting those messages. Photo credits: Chris Luginbuhl
