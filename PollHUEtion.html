PollHUEtion

Summary

Data Visualisation An installation perceived data from YYZ airport departures, controlling three hairdryers to melt crayons by Arduino and p5.js. We aim to turn pollutions into a piece of artwork and to promote this message to people who travel a lot to do more in daily life to set off their carbon prints. Group members: Yiyi Shao, Feng Yuan, Jad Rabbaa
